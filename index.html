Welcome!

I'm a GIS Database Administrator based in Columbus, Ohio, specializing in map creation, process automation, data management, and visualization. I currently work for the Ohio Department of Natural Resources (ODNR).

Ohio Water Withdrawal
Facility Viewer

Designed and implemented two custom geoprocessing tools for this statewide public-facing application: a map printing tool with ODNR branding elements and a data extract tool that enables users to download water withdrawal data in CSV, Excel, or both formats.
